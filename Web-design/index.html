Facebook

Facebook is a social networking site that makes it easy for you to connect and share with family and friends online.

Instagram

Instagram [a] is a photo and video sharing social networking service owned by Meta Platforms. It allows users to upload media that can be edited with filters, be organized by hashtags, and be associated with a location via geographical tagging.

Tweeter

Twitter is a social networking and microblogging online service that allows users to send and receive text-based messages or posts of up to 140 characters.
